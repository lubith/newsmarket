Biography of Punjab 1984

Punjab 1984 is a historical drama film directed by Anurag Singh, released in 2014. The film stars Diljit Dosanjh and Neeru Bajwa in lead roles and is produced by Manmord Sidhu and B. B. Ahuja. Set during a pivotal time in Indian history, the film explores the impact of the insurgency on the lives of ordinary people in Punjab.

Plot Overview

The film revolves around the story of a young man named Hukam Singh, played by Diljit Dosanjh, who is caught in the crossfire of a violent insurgency in Punjab. The narrative unfolds as Hukam navigates through personal loss, family struggles, and the larger societal conflict that plagues the state. The film highlights the emotional and psychological toll of the turbulent times on the people of Punjab.

As Hukam's journey progresses, viewers are taken through a roller-coaster of emotions, showcasing the hardships faced by families during this dark period in Indian history. The film effectively portrays the themes of love, sacrifice, and the quest for justice amidst chaos.
