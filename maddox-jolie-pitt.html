Biography of Maddox Jolie-Pitt

Maddox Chivan Jolie-Pitt was born on August 5, 2001, in Cambodia. He was adopted by Angelina Jolie in 2002 when he was just seven months old. In 2005, after Angelina and Brad Pitt began their relationship, Maddox was adopted by Pitt, making him a part of the Jolie-Pitt family.
