1. Who is Cicely Tyson?

Cicely Tyson was an American actress and model known for her portrayal of strong African-American women in film, television, and theater. Born on December 19, 1924, in Harlem, New York City, she became a prominent figure in the entertainment industry, earning numerous accolades during her career.
