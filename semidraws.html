What are Semidraws?

Semidraws can be defined as a hybrid form of drawing that combines traditional techniques with modern digital tools. This innovative approach allows artists to explore new dimensions in their work, blending realism with abstract elements. The term "semidraw" encompasses a variety of styles and techniques, making it a versatile tool for creatives.

In a semidraw, artists often use a combination of sketching, coloring, and digital manipulation to create unique visual representations. This method not only enhances the aesthetic value of the artwork but also allows for greater experimentation and flexibility in the creative process.
