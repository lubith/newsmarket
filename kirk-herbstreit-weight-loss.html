Biography of Kirk Herbstreit

Kirk Herbstreit was born on August 19, 1969, in Centerville, Ohio. He is a former college football quarterback and current sports analyst, best known for his work with ESPN. A graduate of The Ohio State University, Herbstreit played as a quarterback for the Buckeyes and later transitioned into a career as a sports commentator.
