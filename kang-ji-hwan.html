Biography of Kang Ji Hwan

Kang Ji Hwan, whose real name is Jo Kwang-soo, was born into a modest family. He showed an interest in performing arts from a young age and pursued this passion throughout his education. Kang's early experiences in musical theater laid the foundation for his future in acting.

Early Years and Education

Born in Seongnam, Kang Ji Hwan grew up in a supportive environment that encouraged his artistic pursuits. He attended the Seongnam Arts High School and later pursued a degree in Theater and Film at the Dongguk University. His education played a crucial role in honing his skills and preparing him for a successful career in acting.

Career Highlights

Kang Ji Hwan began his career in the early 2000s, initially appearing in musicals and stage productions. His breakthrough came when he transitioned to television dramas, where he quickly gained popularity. Some of his notable dramas include:

  • 2009: "Lie to Me" - A romantic comedy that showcased his charming persona.
  • 2011: "My Girlfriend is a Gumiho" - A fantasy romance that solidified his status as a leading actor.
  • 2014: "Big Man" - A drama that highlighted his versatility in portraying complex characters.

Notable Works

Kang Ji Hwan's filmography includes a variety of genres, from romantic comedies to action thrillers. Some of his most notable works include:

Awards and Recognition
