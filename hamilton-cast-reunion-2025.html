Biography of Hamilton

Hamilton is a musical that tells the story of Alexander Hamilton, one of the Founding Fathers of the United States, through a contemporary lens. Written by Lin-Manuel Miranda, the production features a diverse cast and a modern score that resonates with today's audiences. The musical has received numerous awards, including the Pulitzer Prize for Drama and multiple Tony Awards.
