Biography of Pauline Musters

Pauline Musters was born on February 26, 1876, in the Netherlands. Standing at just 61 centimeters (24 inches) tall, she was officially recognized as the world's smallest woman by the Guinness World Records. Her petite stature was due to a condition known as primordial dwarfism, which is a rare form of dwarfism that results in a significantly smaller size at birth and throughout life. Despite her physical challenges, Pauline was known for her vibrant personality and remarkable spirit.
