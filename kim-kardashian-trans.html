Early Life and Career

Kim Kardashian was born to Robert Kardashian and Kris Jenner in Los Angeles, California. She gained fame in the early 2000s as a friend of Paris Hilton but rose to prominence with the reality TV show "Keeping Up with the Kardashians," which premiered in 2007. The show chronicled the lives of the Kardashian-Jenner family and became a cultural phenomenon.

Throughout her career, Kim has ventured into various business endeavors, including fashion, beauty, and fragrance lines. Her influence extends beyond television, as she has amassed millions of followers on social media platforms, making her a significant figure in popular culture.
