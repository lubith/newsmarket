Nancy Patricia D'Alesandro Pelosi was born on March 26, 1940, in Baltimore, Maryland. She grew up in a politically active family, which laid the groundwork for her future career in politics. After graduating from Trinity College in Washington, D.C., she began her political journey by volunteering for various Democratic Party initiatives.
