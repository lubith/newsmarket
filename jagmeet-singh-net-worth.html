Biography of Jagmeet Singh

Jagmeet Singh was born on January 2, 1979, in Scarborough, Ontario. His parents immigrated to Canada from India, bringing with them a rich cultural heritage that Singh proudly embraces. He completed his undergraduate studies in biology at the University of Western Ontario and later pursued a law degree at Osgoode Hall Law School.

Political Career

Jagmeet Singh entered politics in 2011 when he was elected as a member of the Ontario Provincial Parliament (MPP) for the Bramalea—Gore—Malton riding. He quickly gained recognition for his passionate advocacy on social issues and was elected as the leader of the NDP in 2017, making history as the first person of a visible minority to lead a major political party in Canada.
