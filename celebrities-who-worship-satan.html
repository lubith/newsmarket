1. Understanding Satanism

Satanism is often misunderstood and misrepresented in popular culture. It is a broad term that encompasses various belief systems, some of which do not even involve the worship of Satan in the traditional sense. Here are some key points to understand:

  • Types of Satanism: There are several branches, including LaVeyan Satanism, which is atheistic, and Theistic Satanism, which involves the worship of Satan as a deity.
  • Philosophical Underpinnings: Many Satanists believe in individualism, self-empowerment, and personal freedom.
  • Misconceptions: Popular media often portrays Satanism as synonymous with criminal behavior or cult activities, which is not the case for most practitioners.
