1. K.G.F: Chapter 2

K.G.F: Chapter 2 continued the saga of Rocky, played by Yash, who rises to power in the Kolar Gold Fields. This film is a sequel to the 2018 blockbuster K.G.F: Chapter 1 and was highly anticipated by fans.

The film features a stellar cast, including Sanjay Dutt and Raveena Tandon, and is directed by Prashanth Neel. Its gripping storyline, combined with high-octane action sequences, made it a box office sensation.

Key Highlights:

  • Release Date: April 14, 2022
  • Genre: Action, Drama
  • Box Office Collection: Over ₹1200 crores

2. Vikrant Rona

Vikrant Rona is a fantasy action-adventure film that features Kichcha Sudeep in the lead role. The film is directed by Anup Bhandari and showcases an intriguing plot filled with mystery and supernatural elements.

Set in a fictional village, the story revolves around a mysterious man who battles dark forces. The film's visuals and sound design received praise, making it a visual treat for audiences.

Key Highlights:

  • Release Date: July 28, 2022
  • Genre: Fantasy, Action
  • Box Office Collection: Over ₹100 crores

3. 777 Charlie

777 Charlie is a heartwarming tale about the unbreakable bond between a man and his dog. Starring Rakshit Shetty, the film is directed by Kiranraj K and has been praised for its emotional storytelling and performances.

This film addresses themes of love, companionship, and the importance of relationships, making it a must-watch for animal lovers and families alike.

Key Highlights:

  • Release Date: June 10, 2022
  • Genre: Drama, Comedy
  • Box Office Collection: Over ₹50 crores

4. Dia

Dia is a romantic drama that beautifully explores the complexities of love and relationships. The film stars Kushee Ravi and is directed by Arun ND. It tells the story of a young woman navigating her emotions and the impact of love on her life.

With its relatable storyline and strong performances, Dia resonated with audiences, particularly the youth, showcasing the intricacies of modern relationships.

Key Highlights:

  • Release Date: February 26, 2022
  • Genre: Romance, Drama
  • Box Office Collection: Over ₹25 crores

From the action-packed K.G.F: Chapter 2 to the emotional journey of 777 Charlie, each film offers something unique to the audience.
