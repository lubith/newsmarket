Biography of Jagmeet Singh

Jagmeet Singh is a prominent political figure in Canada, known for his progressive views and commitment to social equality. He is a member of the House of Commons, representing the riding of Burnaby South. Singh's political journey began in the provincial legislature of Ontario, where he served as a Member of Provincial Parliament (MPP) before ascending to national leadership.
